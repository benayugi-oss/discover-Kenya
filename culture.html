Culture and Food

Kenya has a rich mix of traditions, music, clothing, languages, and food.

Culture

Kenya is home to many ethnic groups, each with its own traditions and customs. Music, dance, storytelling, and community are important parts of life.

Popular Foods

  1. Ugali
  2. Nyama Choma
  3. Sukuma Wiki
  4. Chapati

Kenyan food is flavorful, filling, and often shared with family and friends.

Fun Facts

• Kenya is famous for long-distance runners.

• Swahili and English are official languages.

• Kenya has both savannas and beautiful coastlines.
